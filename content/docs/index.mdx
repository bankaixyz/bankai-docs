title: Overview
description: Bankai gives you trustless access to on-chain data
  through stateless light clients and recursive proofs -- verify
  anywhere, no infrastructure required.
---

Bankai is a stateless light-client system. It syncs light clients
fully off-chain, compresses their trusted state into recursive zk
proofs, and lets you verify headers and chain data on demand --
on-chain, client-side, or inside a zkVM. No contracts to deploy, no
sync to maintain, no chain-specific infrastructure to run.

If you're new here, start with the concepts to understand the
architecture, then move to the SDK to build your first proof flow.
